Lego Star Wars Review

Review done 3/20/2017 by

Durias Thomas

Lego star wars is a kids friendly lego game that stays consistent with the maovies. They have subtle jokes for adults to enjoy. Some of the levels can feel restricting compared to an open world game, but they make it work well with their gameplay mechanics.
